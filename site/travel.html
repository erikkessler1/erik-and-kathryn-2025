Location

Most of the wedding festivities will happen on Erik's parent's property at 948 Ladd Rd. Pownal, VT. Lodging and dining options are greater just over the state boarder in Williamstown, MA, or North Adams, MA (10-15 minutes away).

Getting Here

For those flying, the nearest commercial airport is the Albany International Airport, which is around an hour away. The Boston Logan International Airport is around 3 hours away, but has more flight options. Renting a car to get from either airport to Pownal/Williamstown is likely the best option.

Nearby Amtrak train stations include Albany-Rensselaer, NY (~1 hour away), and Pittsfield, MA (40 minutes away).

Unfortunately, rideshare app options (Uber and Lyft) are limited in this area so we strongly encourage renting a car or coordinating with other guests. If this becomes an obstacle in your attendance, please let us know.

Suggested Lodging

We will not be reserving a room block. There are several AirBnB and VRBO options in the area (Pownal, VT, or Williamstown, MA).
Potential hotels include:
  • Fairfield Inn and Suites Williamstown
  • The William's Inn
  • Tourists
  • Cozy Corner Motel
  • Villager Motel

Day-Of Transportation

Parking at the wedding venue will be limited, so we ask that you carpool, if possible.

If there is interest, we may look to charter transportation to and from one of the hotels. If you are interested in this option please indicate so on your RSVP or reach out to Erik or Kathryn directly.
